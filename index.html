Gouraud Shading of Sievert’s Surface – Variant 18

Student: Andrii Khavkin

The goal of Practical Assignment №2 is to extend the geometrical model from PA1 by implementing a full WebGL shading pipeline and rendering the analytic Sievert’s Surface using Gouraud shading with dynamic lighting.

All rendering is performed using WebGL 1.0, without external libraries, except matrix utilities provided in the course skeleton.

Drag your mouse on the cube to rotate it.
(On a touch screen, you can use your finger.)
